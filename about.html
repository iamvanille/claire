Our Story

The Iconic began with, you guessed it, a love for all things travel.

With 8+ years of experience in the luxury tourism industry, we have a well-established network of important partners and stakeholders to facilitate a seamless and awe-inspiring travel experience for our clients.

Without fail, we always go above and beyond our client’s expectations.
